About Me

Turning educational data into rigorous, usable insight.

My primary research areas include educational psychology, psychometrics, and quantitative research methods. I apply measurement and statistical methods to learning and teaching analytics, with the goal of understanding how learning processes, psychological characteristics, and educational outcomes are connected.
